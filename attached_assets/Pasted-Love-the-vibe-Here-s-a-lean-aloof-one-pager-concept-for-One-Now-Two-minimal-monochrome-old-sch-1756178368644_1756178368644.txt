Love the vibe. Here’s a lean, “aloof” one-pager concept for One Now Two—minimal, monochrome, old-school serif, and laser-focused on showcasing work with just enough copy to intrigue.

Concept (for your Replit build)

Look & feel

Monochrome: black / white / soft grey (#111, #f7f7f7, #9ca3af).

Typeface: old-school serif for titles (Cormorant Garamond), neutral grotesk for microcopy (Inter).

Big whitespace, slow fades, no clutter. Feels a bit exclusive on purpose.

Tone

Sparse, confident, slightly mysterious. You’re the prize. Minimal words; let the films do the talking.

Structure (single page)

Hero: Autoplay, muted, looping mashup reel (poster image fallback). Centered logotype + tiny line. Single CTA: “Enquire”.

Why: 2–3 lines about moving from TVCs/corporate (Young Buck Media) into weddings—calm on the day, meticulous in the edit.

Selected Works: 6 films only. Black-and-white thumbnails that gently fade to colour on hover. Captions: Couple — Venue — Year. Click → Vimeo/YouTube.

Ethos: Three ultra-short lines (no bullet bloat). Example: “Observe quietly. Edit honestly. Leave space for feeling.”

Availability CTA: One line: “Dates are limited.” + mailto link.

Footer: Tiny, centred. Sydney • NSW. © One Now Two.

Micro-interactions

Slow fade-ins (CSS only).

Cursor hover → grayscale fades to colour on thumbnails.

No heavy animations; keep it elegant.
